# Prompt Engineering Reflection

## What worked:
Developing the prompts for the personas was a difficult task. I had to research through many youtube videos and the linkedin of the Instructors. 
For kshitij sir's behaviour and talking style I was quite familier as he have taught us so it was easier to make changes in the prompts. 
For anshuman sir and abhimanyu sir persona I had to watch several youtube videos and read through serveral of their posts to create their persona prompts. 


## What the GIGO principle taught me:
Garbage In, Garbage Out was immediately apparent during iterative development. When initially relying on vague instructions like "Act like Anshuman," the output became a vague solutions without building the underlying theoretical knowledge. By tightly constraining the prompt and providing concrete examples within the instructions, the outputs transformed from "garbage" (generic answers) to precise, persona-aligned advice that emphasized fundamental understanding over immediate copy-paste solutions.

## What I would improve:
To improve the project further more and make the responses of the AI more accurate I would implement RAG in the project, since sending a lot of files with each request is not optimal. so with RAG I can retrieve relevent information from a large number of files to generate more accurate responses. 
And I would also add a button to convert the text to speech with the person's voice using elevelabs.
